Meet the Composer

  • Orion Palmer is a graduate of Augusta University and Valdosta State University where he studied and received a Bachelor and Masters in Music Education.
  • He has been the recipient of the 2015 GA Music Teacher's National Association Young Artist Composer Competition Award and was recognized as the by Chukker Creek Elementary School as 2021-2022 Teacher of the Year.
  • In addition to this Orion Palmer has worked with student filmmakers in the productions and contributing his music to help bring their films to life.
